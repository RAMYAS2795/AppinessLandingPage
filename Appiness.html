What are the types of bone marrow transplant?

Autologous bone marrow transplant- Marrow cells are collected from the patient's own body and transfused back after high dose chemotheraphy.

Allogenic Bone marrow transplant(Matched sibling Donor- Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with maroow from a genetically(HLA)matched sibling donor.

Allogenic Bone marrow transplant(Matched Unrelated Donor)- Replacement of diseased or or defective marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched donor obtained from bone marrow registries in India and abroad.

Haplo-identical Bone marrow transplant - Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from the parent.

Umbilical cord Bone marrow transaplant- Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from genetically (HLA) matched umbilical cord donor obtained from umbilical cord registers in India and abroad.

What are the conditions that require bone marrow transplant?

BMT is the treatment of choice for blood disorders like Thalassemia,Sickle cell anemia,bone marrow failure syndromes like Aplastic anemia, serious immune difiencies and certain genetic metabolic disorders.

It is also used to treat high risk cancers like some cases of Acute Lymphoblastic Luekemia,Acute Myeloid Luekemia,Multiple Myeloma and solid tumours like Nueroblastoma, Ewngs Sarcoma and certain relapsed cancers like Lymphomas
